Research & Projects

Luzero

The Berkeley Energy group’s Luzero project created the Luzero device - a home energy monitor that nonintrusively disaggregates plug load energy consumption and analyzes the data to generate meaningful trends and tips on behavioral energy consumption

While working on the project, I was involved with both the material selection and the manufacturing process design for the Luzero console. In addition, I created d3.js screens to display the aggregated data in an intuitive and user-friendly manner.

DuraTact

DuraTact is a specially designed surface that reduces damage caused in collisions. It is a defect engineering experiment that revolves around the idea of strategically weakening a surface. By engineering defects into the surface, it is possible to control the mechanism through which DuraTact fails. This in turn allows us to reduce damages by preserving vital components of our device.

Lazr Labs

Lazr labs is an ongoing effort involving designing and modelling RC Aviation parts, especially quadrotor frames, and laser cutting them to create actual components. These frames can be combined with a variety of third party electronics to create functioning quadrotors. Further information about this project can be found at:

Lazr.us

I am also currently developing an e-commerce website to market the products created by Lazr labs. This website will contain a full list of Lazr products including the styx frames and will be deployed in March 2017.

BlueWater

BlueWater is design for a tool to correct excessive water-wasting habits. Its primary objective is to reduce water wastage in both residences and industries. In addition to measuring water consumption, BlueWater provides detailed reports about which outlets, activities and time periods lead to the most usage of water. Moreover, BlueWater compares the user’s consumption levels with those of other users in the region and suggests plans for future water use. By using readily accessible technology such as arduino, smartphones and wifi connectivity, BlueWater forms a low-cost, innovative and unique solution to a serious environmental problem.
